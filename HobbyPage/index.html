Catchiball


The Game Cachibol is a team ball game based on the rules of volleyball and takes place on a plot identical to that of Volleyball (9x18 m) with a fundamental difference – in mamanet the players must catch the ball and not just hit. This allows mother -players of all ages with little or no background in team sport to be able to participate. Each team consists of up to 14 players, 1 coach and who meet once a week for team practice and once a week for league game. The game has rules & regulations that can be found in Mamanet website, The Season starts & ends with the school year.
